Get to know me!

I am Windelyn Selma Santiago, a 20-year-old extrovert who thrives on learning through conversation. Music is a core part of my identity; I am a passionate singer and guitarist, serving as a Sunday School teacher, back-up singer, and guitarist in my church ministry.
